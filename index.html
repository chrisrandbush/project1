What is Beer Here

Have you ever been to a professional sporting event and heard the vendors walking up and down the aisles yelling "Beer Here! Get your ice cold beer here!"? The mixture of an ice cold beer and sports is something that cannot be replicated anywhere else. Beer Here is a site created for users to find their post-quarantine watering hole, or micro-watering hole, and also see when their favorite teams are playing that same day. This app shows the users no only what breweries are in your U.S. city of choice, but also where they are on a map down to their exact longitude and latitude.

Why did we build this?

We created Beer Here to help users find new and fun breweries in their city of choice, allowing them to support these local brew-houses and cheer on their team with others around them. The beer industry has been going through a sort of renaissance over the last few years, and as a team of craft beer lovers, we could not think of a better way to help that industry grow more in popularity than to put the users one search away from trying a new brew. We hope you enjoy our creation!
